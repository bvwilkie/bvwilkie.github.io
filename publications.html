Publications and presentations

As an academic historian, I have published research primarily in the areas of Australian environmental history and on the history of the Scots in Australia. I have also written for a variety of magazines and outlets, including The Guardian, Meanjin, The Spectator, Quadrant, ABC's The Drum, History Scotland, The Scottish Banner, and Scotland Now, on current affairs, history, and music. I also blog regularly on a range of topics. With some exceptions, the following list is focused on scholarly work.
