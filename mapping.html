Mapping Frontier Violence

I am currently compiling a range of primary and secondary historical sources and building a dataset that will form the basis of an interactive map of Indigenous-European frontier conflict in nineteenth-century Australia. The first stage of the project will focus on Victoria, and particularly Western Victoria. The map displayed below is an early example from this project, and displays recorded killings and massacres of Dhauwurd wurrung, or Gunditjmara, people in south-west Victoria.
