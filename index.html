About Me

I am an Australian historian, writer, and academic. My research focuses on histories of Australia's society and environment. My first book is The Scots in Australia 1788-1938, and I am currently writing The Grampians: History & Nature. In addition to academic and popular histories, I write on Australian arts, culture, and politics for a variety of publications, and I am a regular music reviewer for Limelight Magazine.
