About Me

I am an Australian historian and writer. My research focuses on histories of Australia's society and environment, and I currently teach in the Australian Studies programme at Deakin University. In addition to academic and popular histories, I regularly write on Australian arts, culture, and politics for a variety of publications. My first book is The Scots in Australia 1788-1938.
